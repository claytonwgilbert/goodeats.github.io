Ooey Gooey Brownies
Ingredients
Single Recipe Doubled Ingredients
1 1/2 cup 3 cups Granulated Sugar
3/4 cup 1 1/2 cup All-Purpose Flour
2/3 cup 1 1/3 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
1/2 cup 1 cup Powdered Sugar
1/2 cup 1 cup Dark Chocolate Chip Morsels
1/2 teaspoon 1 teaspoon Sea Salt
2 large 4 large Eggs
1/2 cup 1 cup Canola Oil
2 tablespoons 4 tablespoons Water
1/2 teaspoon 1 teaspoon Vanilla
Recipe
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Lightly oil 8x8 baking pan
  3. Combine sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, morsels, salt into medium bowl. Mix well
  4. Whisk together eggs, canola oil, water, and vanilla into large bowl separately
  5. Mix dry mixture into wet mixture(medium bowl into large bowl). Stir until combined
  6. Pour batter into pan and smooth
  7. Bake for 45 minutes or until center is dry with toothpick
  8. Cool completely until slicing
